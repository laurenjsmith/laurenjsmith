Welcome

Let Me Tell You Why I Do What I Do

Let me start with a truth that breaks my heart every single day: the vast majority of books that are published — books that took months or years of blood, sweat, and tears to write — will sell fewer than 250 copies in their lifetime. Not because they're bad books. Not because the stories aren't compelling. But because the authors behind those books didn't have the marketing knowledge, strategic guidance, or industry expertise needed to get their work in front of the right readers.

"I am a strategic, data-driven, creatively-minded marketing professional who has dedicated my career to understanding the book industry inside and out."

My name is Lauren J. Smith, and I am a Book Marketing Expert. I'm not someone who simply posts a few things on social media and calls it marketing. I'm not someone who offers vague advice like 'build your platform' without telling you exactly how to do it. And I'm certainly not someone who uses a one-size-fits-all template and hopes for the best.

I know how Amazon's algorithm works. I know how to craft book descriptions that convert browsers into buyers. I know how to build email lists that generate sales. I know how to create social media strategies that turn casual scrollers into devoted fans. I know how to secure media coverage that puts your book in front of new audiences. And I know how to do all of this in a way that's authentic to who you are as an author.

I've had the privilege of working with authors across every genre imaginable — from romance novels to political non-fiction, from children's picture books to academic textbooks, from heart-pounding thrillers to soul-stirring poetry collections.

I don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Every book is unique, every author is unique, and every marketing plan I create is tailored specifically to your goals, your genre, and your audience. I don't make outlandish promises or guarantee overnight success — because real, sustainable book marketing doesn't work that way. What I do promise is that I will bring my full expertise, creativity, and dedication to your project.

Common Challenges Authors Face

"I have no idea where to start"

The overwhelm of countless marketing opinions and strategies leads to paralysis.

"I'm spending money on ads but not seeing results"

High cost-per-click, low conversion rates, and spending money without meaningful return.

"I'm posting on social media but nobody's engaging"

Showing up every day but getting minimal likes, no comments, and no growth.

"I don't have an email list"

Zero subscribers and no idea how to get people to sign up.

"My book is published but not selling"

You invested in editing, cover design, and formatting, but the book sits with barely any sales.

"I'm not getting any reviews"

Without reviews, readers hesitate. Without sales, you can't get reviews.

"I don't know how to price my book"

Conflicting advice about pricing strategies and worry that the wrong price is hurting sales.

"I don't have time for marketing"

Juggling writing, work, family, and life. Adding comprehensive marketing feels impossible.

"I can't get any media coverage"

Press releases go unanswered, blogger pitches get ignored.

"Nothing seems to work"

You've tried different tactics but still aren't seeing results.

My Story

Hi there! I'm Lauren J. Smith, and I'm glad you're here. 👋

My journey into book marketing started with a simple love of books. For as long as I can remember, I've been an avid reader. Growing up, you'd rarely find me without a book in my hand.

But as I grew older, I discovered another passion: marketing. I was fascinated by the psychology behind why people buy, how brands build loyalty, and what makes a message resonate.

Then one day, it hit me — why not combine my two greatest passions? Why not use my marketing expertise to help the people I admire most: authors?

And that's how my book marketing career began.

Since then, I've had the privilege of working with authors across every genre imaginable. I've helped debut novelists navigate the world of self-publishing. I've worked with traditionally published authors looking to supplement their publisher's marketing efforts.

Every author I work with has a unique story, a unique voice, and a unique set of challenges. That's why I never use cookie-cutter strategies.

"I'll be honest with you: I can't guarantee that your book will become a bestseller. No marketer can honestly make that promise. What I can guarantee is that I will bring my full expertise, creativity, and dedication to your project."

I will develop a strategy grounded in research and data. I will execute with consistency and attention to detail. And I will be transparent with you every step of the way.

What drives me every day is the belief that great books deserve the opportunity to find their readers.

When I'm not helping authors with their marketing, you can find me curled up with a good book, exploring the latest marketing trends, or enjoying a cup of coffee at my favorite café. ☕📚
